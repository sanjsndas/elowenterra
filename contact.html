Quick Wisdom — Before You Reach

🌼 How do I schedule a personalized garden design?

Simply fill the form with “Garden Consultation” as subject. One of our design keepers will contact you for a virtual walk or on-site visit. Basic consultation includes soil mapping and golden palette blueprint for $85.

🍊 Do you ship marigold & sunflower seeds worldwide?

Yes! We ship heirloom, organic seeds to most temperate regions. Please note customs restrictions. Check our Seed Shop section for more details and phytosanitary certificates.

🌿 Can I visit the Terra Sanctum in person?

Absolutely. The Hollowbrook Valley sanctuary is open for guided tours every Friday & Saturday. Please send a message 48h in advance — we welcome small pilgrimages.

🧑‍🌾 I'm a beekeeper — any orange flowers for nectar?

Our signature 'Amber Fire' Zinnias and Mexican Sunflower (Tithonia) are bee magnets. We offer bulk pollinator packs. Contact our team for wholesale pricing.
