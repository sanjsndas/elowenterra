Privacy is Sacred Soil

Your personal data is treated like a rare heirloom seed — protected, respected, and never exploited. Read how we nurture confidentiality in the digital garden.

Last updated: March 15, 2026 Version 2.1 — Golden Harvest

Elowen Terra Sanctum (“we”, “us”, “the Garden Keepers”) is rooted in the belief that transparency is as vital as sunlight. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (elowenterrasanctum.garden) or engage with our horticultural services, consultations, newsletters, and seed offerings. By interacting with our sanctum, you trust us with your story — we vow to honor that trust.

Our Golden Pledge: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your information is used solely to cultivate a better experience, respond to your inquiries, and send you the garden wisdom you request — nothing more.

1. Information We Gather

🌼 Information you share directly: When you fill out our contact form, subscribe to “The Solar Almanac”, register for a workshop, or request a garden consultation, we collect details such as your name, email address, postal address (if needed for seed shipping), phone number, and message content. For purchases or donations, we collect billing information (processed via secure third-party gateways – we never store full payment details on our servers).

🍊 Automatic information: Like most digital gardens, we gather basic usage data via cookies and similar technologies: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring website, and timestamps. This helps us understand which plants (pages) are most loved by our visitors and improve our layout. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

🌻 Information from third parties: If you interact with our social media channels (Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube), we may receive aggregated insights — never private direct messages without your consent.

2. How We Nourish Your Data

We use the information we collect only for rooted and legitimate purposes, including:

  • ✔ Responding to your messages, questions, or consultation requests (usually within 2–3 sunrises, as promised).
  • ✔ Sending you seasonal gardening guides, orange-bloom tips, and event invitations — only if you’ve opted in. You can unsubscribe anytime with a single click.
  • ✔ Processing seed orders, sanctuary visits, or workshop registrations.
  • ✔ Improving our website’s performance and user experience (like analyzing which plant articles thrive best).
  • ✔ Protecting the security of our community — for example, detecting fraudulent activity or spam.

Legal basis (for GDPR & CCPA friends): We rely on your consent (e.g., newsletter signup), contractual necessity (e.g., fulfilling a seed order), and legitimate interests (e.g., analytics to enhance our website). You can withdraw consent anytime.

3. Sharing & Disclosure — The Sacred Boundary

We never sell your personal information. However, we may share limited data with trusted partners who help us operate the garden:

  • Service Providers: Email delivery platforms (e.g., for “The Solar Almanac”), secure payment processors, and e-commerce tools. These parties are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and use it solely for the services they provide on our behalf.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of Elowen Terra Sanctum, our visitors, or the physical garden spaces.
  • Business transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, you will be notified via email and/or prominent notice on our site before any data transfer.
Keeper’s Promise: We never share your personal details with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for their own marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Digital Footprints

Our sanctuary uses essential and functional cookies to remember your preferences and analyze general traffic patterns. You can manage cookie settings through your browser. Third-party cookies (from embedded videos or social sharing) are minimal and respect your privacy. Please see our detailed Cookies Policy for the full flavor.

5. Data Retention — Seasonal Cycles

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. For example:

  • • Contact form messages: stored for up to 24 months after last interaction, then anonymized.
  • • Newsletter subscribers: data kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • • Transaction records: kept for 7 years for tax/legal reasons (only essential details retained).

If you wish to have your data “returned to the earth” (deleted), just contact our Keepers — we will honour your request promptly, unless retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights — The Gardener’s Prerogative

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • 🔹 Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • 🔹 Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • 🔹 Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Ask us to delete your personal data.
  • 🔹 Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • 🔹 Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • 🔹 Right to Object: Opt-out of direct marketing or certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@elowenterrasanctum.garden or use the contact form on this site. We will respond within 30 days (usually sooner, like a sprouting radish). We never discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

7. Children’s Privacy — Little Sprouts

Our website and services are directed toward adults and gardening enthusiasts of all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security Measures — The Iron Gardener’s Gate

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including SSL encryption, restricted access to databases, regular security audits, and staff training on data protection. While no online transmission is 100% secure, we strive to use industry-leading practices to safeguard your information. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and regulators as required by applicable laws.

9. International Transfers

Our Sanctum is based in the Hollowbrook Valley (USA), but we welcome visitors and customers from across the globe. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our servers reside. By using our services, you consent to this transfer. For European users, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Changes to This Policy (Seasonal Updates)

As our garden grows, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any revisions on this page with a new “Last updated” date. For significant changes, we may also send a notice via email or a banner on the website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
