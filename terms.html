Our Sacred Terms & Privacy

Rooted in transparency, respect, and the golden vow to protect all who wander into our digital sanctuary.

1. Terms of Use — The Gardeners’ Covenant

Welcome to Elowen Terra Sanctum (“we”, “us”, “our”, “Sanctum”). By accessing our website, purchasing seeds, joining workshops, or engaging with any digital content, you agree to honor the spirit of this sacred space. These terms are designed to nurture trust, respect intellectual blooms, and ensure that our community remains vibrant and safe.

  • 🌼 Use of Content: All text, images, plant illustrations, and design are the soulful property of Elowen Terra Sanctum or its licensors. You may share blog posts or garden guides for personal, non-commercial use provided you credit our sanctuary with a link back. No mining of our seed databases or mass extraction of planting calendars without written permission.
  • 🧑‍🌾 Community Standards: Our forums, events, and comment sections are pollinators’ meadows — be kind, be curious. Harassment, hate speech, or any behavior that wilts our inclusive ethos will result in immediate restriction from membership programs and garden access.
  • 🛒 Purchases & Donations: When you adopt seeds, purchase from our apothecary, or donate to the Amber Fund, you enter a covenant of care. All sales support land restoration and tuition-free programs. Digital products (certifications, guides) are non-refundable but exchangeable for another digital blessing within 14 days.
  • 📬 Accuracy of Information: We till the soil of truth. While we strive for botanical accuracy, nature is wild. We are not liable for crop failure or variations in bloom color due to microclimates. Always consult your local growing zone — our golden advice is a companion, not a guarantee.

2. Privacy Policy — How We Guard Your Embers

Your privacy is like an heirloom seed: precious, irreplaceable, and never to be mishandled. This policy explains what information we gather, how we use it, and the choices you hold as a member of our growing circle.

Information We Collect

  • 🌱 Personal Data: When you sign up for newsletters, purchase seeds, register for “Solar Horticulture Certification” or RSVP to the Fête of Eternal Ember, we collect your name, email address, postal address (for seed deliveries), and payment information (securely processed through trusted gateways).
  • 🍂 Non-Personal Data: Like the wind through sunflowers, we gather anonymous analytics — pages visited, time spent in our digital labyrinth, referral sources — through cookies and similar technologies. This helps us improve the garden path for all pilgrims.
  • 🌻 Sensitive Inferences: We never request health data unless you voluntarily enroll in therapeutic horticulture programs (e.g., veterans’ grief support). Any such data stays strictly within our confidential keepers’ logs and is deleted after program completion.

How We Use Your Information

To process donations and seed orders, send seasonal planting guidance, notify you of upcoming solstice gatherings, improve our website’s bloom (user experience), and — only with explicit consent — share stories of impact with our global fellowship. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data for honey or gold. Ever.

Data Protection & Retention

We use SSL encryption, restricted-access databases, and annual privacy audits. Payment info is tokenized and never stored on our own servers. Most records are retained for 3 years unless you request erasure earlier. You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data by emailing keepers@elowenterrasanctum.garden.

3. Cookies Policy — Nourishing Tiny Seeds of Data

Our website uses cookies (small text files placed on your device) to enhance your journey, remember preferences, and understand how visitors interact with our golden grove. Just as pollinators remember the sweetest blossoms, cookies recall your language and cart contents.

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for navigation, secure checkout, and accessing members-only areas. Cannot be disabled without breaking the garden gate.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your favorite seed categories, preferred measurement units (imperial/metric), and whether you’ve already seen our solstice announcement.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use privacy-forward tools (Fathom or Matomo) to count unique visitors and popular pages — no cross-site tracking or creepy profiling.
  • Managing Cookies: You can adjust cookie settings in your browser. However, disabling functional cookies may dim your experience — some personalized recommendations (like your “Golden Hour planting guide”) may not appear.
Transparency note: We do not use third-party advertising cookies. No retargeting ads follow you after leaving our sanctuary. Your digital footprint stays within the sanctum's walls.

4. Your Rights Under GDPR / CCPA / Global Garden Norms

Elowen Terra Sanctum welcomes gardeners from every continent. If you reside in the European Economic Area, California, or other regions with specific privacy laws, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (free of charge once per season).
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data when it’s no longer necessary for the purposes collected.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Opt out of processing for direct marketing or analytics at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Keeper at privacy@elowenterrasanctum.garden or by post: 127 Goldenvein Lane, Hollowbrook Valley, HV 87012. We will respond within 30 days (usually sooner, like a marigold sprout in May).
