Cookies Policy

Sweet transparency with every crumb — we use only intentional cookies to enhance your garden journey, never to harvest your privacy.

Last updated: March 15, 2026 Cookie harvest — ethical & minimal

Elowen Terra Sanctum (“we”, “us”, “Garden Keepers”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to cultivate a warm, functional, and personalized experience. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we plant on your device, and how you can manage your cookie preferences — because you are the true steward of your digital soil.

Our philosophy: We treat cookies like companion plants — some are essential for growth (functional), others help us understand our ecosystem (analytics). We never use invasive “malicious” cookies or share data with unannounced third parties. No surprise ingredients.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your computer, tablet, or smartphone when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (like language, login state, or shopping cart) over a period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them each visit. Think of them as small biodegradable tags that make your journey smoother.

3. Detailed Cookie Inventory

Below is a specific breakdown of individual cookies we commonly place when you interact with Elowen Terra Sanctum. This list is updated seasonally.

  • cookie_consent_accepted — Stores your cookie preference (essential, analytics, etc.). Expires in 1 year.
  • session_id (strictly necessary) — Used for server session management, such as contact form submission. Expires when you close your browser.
  • _ga, _ga_* (Google Analytics) — Helps us measure traffic and understand popular content. IP addresses are anonymized. Data retention: 26 months. You can opt out via our opt-out mechanism or browser extensions.
  • gardener_pref — Remembers if you prefer dark/light mode or larger font (accessibility). Expires in 6 months.
  • last_visit_timestamp — Functional analytics to greet returning visitors. Expires after 30 days.
Third-party Cookie disclosure: Our site may embed maps (OpenStreetMap) or video content from YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode. Those services may set cookies, but we limit their activation until user consent or click-to-load. See Google's Cookie Policy for details.

4. Your Cookie Choices — Control Panel

You are the head gardener of your browser. You can manage or delete cookies in several ways:

  • ✧ Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit, we display a lightweight banner allowing you to accept only necessary cookies or opt-in to analytics. Your preference is stored for 12 months.
  • ✧ Browser settings: Most web browsers let you block or delete all cookies, or set alerts for when cookies are placed. Instructions vary: look under “privacy” or “site settings”.
  • ✧ Global opt-out tools: You can enable “Do Not Track” (DNT) in your browser. We honor DNT signals by disabling non-essential analytics.
  • ✧ Google Analytics Opt-out: Download the official Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookies & Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 through cookies. Any analytics data is aggregated and does not identify individual users regardless of age. Parents or guardians can delete cookie files using browser tools at any time.

6. Security & Cookie Integrity

All cookies we set are either “HttpOnly” (where possible) or secured with SameSite attributes to reduce risk of cross-site scripting. We never store sensitive personal data — like credit card details or passwords — inside cookies. Your security is part of our sacred practice.

7. Updates to This Cookies Policy

We may refresh this policy to align with new regulations or changes in our cookie usage. The “Last updated” date at the top will always reflect the latest revision. If we introduce new cookie categories, we will notify you via a prominent notice or email. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance, but you always retain control through your browser settings.
